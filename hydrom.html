How we Made it:Hydroelectric Generator

This unique model is made from various materials. At the bottom is a container filled with water. To the left of the container is a stand holding a can/bottle, which is filled with water.

A straw leads out of the bottle, which would drain the water from the can/bottle.The can/bottle acts as a reservoir. To the bottom right of the straw is a water wheel, which when the water is drained through the straw, would spin.

A hobby dynamo is paired with the water wheel. It will convert mechanic energy, produced by the spinning water wheel, into electrical energy.
